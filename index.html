Foodify is led by Doris Todorovic and a talented team of chefs, assistants, sommeliers, wait staff and hosts. We serve refined contemporary food with friendly, attentive service in a luxurious dining space overlooking Dublin’s magnificent Phoenix park. Doris is working closely with Ireland’s most dedicated farmers and food producers to create a series of unique and exciting dishes.
